    Meet the Cocary Hotel!

    Close your eyes and imagine paradise. A place in the middle of the Atlantic Forest and bathed by the sea, where you find peace, tranquility, comfort and convenience.

    Cocaray's only hotel foot on the sand

    Set in a completely wooded area, Hotel Cocaray the only foot on the sand hotel. Here the sea will be your companion and you will have the beauties of nature as a guide.

    • Location

      Located in southern Bahishy, between the sea and the Atlantic Forest, Cocaray has refreshing waterfalls, beautiful beaches, many of them so small that they don't even appear on maps.

    • Packages and Offers

      Do you know that laid-back date with family and friends? Or a moment relax? Our structure has a diversity of spaces that provide unforgettable moments.

    • Accommodations

      Hotel Cocaray has 29 units, divided between 11 chalets, 16 large suites and 2 family chalets, all with cable TV, minibar, and balcony with hammock.
